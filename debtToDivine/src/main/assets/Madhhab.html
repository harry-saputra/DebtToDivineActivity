Madhhab Information

  • The importance and relevance of madhhabs can be read about here.
  • The madhhab you select will determine some minor differences in making up Prayers or other missed worship.
  • Currently the app only supports Hanafi and Shafii madhhabs, inshaAllah future versions may be expanded to include the other two, namely Maliki and Hanbali.
  • Prayers
    • Hanafi
      • (PRO version) When tracking individual prayers, Witr is included.
      • Lochia maximum is 40 days.
    • Shafii
      • (PRO version) When tracking individual prayers, Witr is excluded.
      • Lochia maximum is 60 days.
  • Fasts
    • Hanafi
      • (PRO version) No Fidya for delayed make ups -- Coming Soon.
    • Shafii
      • (PRO version) Calculate Fidya for delayed make ups -- Coming Soon.
  • Expiation (Kaffara) -- Coming Soon
    • Hanafi
      • Expiation is due from Male and Female alike.
      • Only a maximum of one expiation.
    • Shafii
      • Expiation is only due from Males.
      • Can be multiple expiations.
  • NOTE:
    • Changing your Madhhab will reset your Witr progress.
    • PLEASE revise the rulings of your madhhab, you may think that you need to (or don't need to) make up certain acts of worship or pay a fidya for breaking your fast a certain way and you may be doing more or less than you need to.
    • This app is designed to HELP you track your debt, it is not a pocket mufti, if in doubt consult a local Imam or seek answers below:
