Gender Information

  • Here you select your gender (Male or Female).
  • Depending on what your gender is, some options will be available, others will not be.
  • Female
    • Will allow menstruation setting for missed prayer debt.
    • Will not allow Expiation type if Shafii madhhab is chosen.
    • When calculating the deadline date, and average menstruation rate of 5 days per month is taken into account.
    • e.g. if a woman has 2 months debt, she selects to make up 1 day per day, she will have 70 days to make up the debt rather than 60 as it took into account that in those 2 months she may menstruate an average of 5 days per month.
    • This also works when a woman selects a date of deadline, the Days per day value is adjusted to show how many days per day she would need to pray considering she may menstruate an average of 5 days per month during the time of her deadline.
  • Male
    • Will allow Expiation type if Shafii madhhab is chosen.
  • NOTE:
    • Changing the gender setting will reset any menstruation values.
