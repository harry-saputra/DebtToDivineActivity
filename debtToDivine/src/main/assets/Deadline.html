Deadline Information

  • Here you can select the deadline by which you wish to make up your total debt.
  • When you have selected your deadline date, the home page will display how many days remaining till the deadline, and it will also display how many days worth of prayers you need to pray in order to complete your debt by the deadline.
  • When making up missed fasts, you will only ever see 1 day per day on the home screen as you cannot fasts more than 1 missed fast day each day.
  • You may not select a deadline date before "today".
