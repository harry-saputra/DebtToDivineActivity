Debt Total Information

  • Here you select your total debt owed to Allah (Mighty and Majestic is He).
  • Before setting this value, think back to the times you most likely would have missed prayers or fasts and write down how many days, months and/or years you believe you owe. Add a little more to that value to give yourself certainty that making up this amount will clear all your debt.
  • Prayer and Fasting
    • The dialog that appears when you have chosen a Prayer or Fasting debt type will have 3 bars:
    • The first bar you can select the number of years you wish to make up.
    • The second bar you can select the number of months you wish to make up.
    • The third bar you can select the number of days you wish to make up.
    • The app will tally up the total number of days and this will be displayed on the home page.
  • NOTE:
    • You cannot set a total debt less than what you have already made up, the selection wheel will auto revert to the lowest possible debt total when you try to select anything less.
