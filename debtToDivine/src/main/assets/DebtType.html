Debt Type Information

  • Here you select the type of debt you wish to make up or pay back.
  • Different debt types change the way the app functions:
  • Prayers
    • (PRO Version) Displays a Prayers Menu Option when clicking menu that allows you to track individual make up prayers.
    • If you are a female and have selected the Female gender in the app, you will be allowed to select the number of Menses and Lochia days you wish to calculate and subtract from the total debt.
  • Fasting
    • (PRO Version) Displays a Fidya Menu Option when clicking menu that allows you to calculate and track the amount of fidya that you may owe because of missed fasts. -- Coming Soon
  • Expiation (Kaffarah) -- Coming Soon
  • Monetary -- Coming Soon
  • NOTE:
    • Changing the debt type will force a confirmation message and will ask whether you would like to clear your debt, menses and completed total.
